Let's Talk
What is estate planning, exactly?
Estate planning is the process of arranging for the management and distribution of your assets during your lifetime and after death. It includes wills, trusts, powers of attorney, and healthcare directives.
Do I really need a trust?
Not everyone needs a trust, but most families benefit from one. A revocable living trust can help you avoid probate, maintain privacy, and ensure a smoother transfer of assets to your beneficiaries.
How long does the process take?
A typical estate plan takes 2-4 weeks from initial consultation to signed documents. Complex estates or business succession plans may take longer depending on the scope.
What happens if I don't have a plan?
Without a plan, state law determines how your assets are distributed — which may not align with your wishes. Your family may face lengthy probate proceedings, higher costs, and unnecessary stress.
Can I update my plan later?
Absolutely. Life changes — marriages, births, divorces, new assets — all warrant a plan review. We offer annual check-ins and can amend your documents as needed.
What's included in the free consultation?
