What Is the Estate Planning Process?
We start with a free consultation to understand your family's needs. From there, Lady Jane drafts your personalized documents — wills, trusts, powers of attorney — and walks you through every detail before finalizing. The entire process is designed to be simple, clear, and stress-free.
How Much Does Estate Planning Cost?
Pricing depends on the complexity of your estate, but our plans start at transparent flat rates with no hidden fees. Whether you need a simple will or a comprehensive trust package, we'll give you a clear quote upfront so there are never any surprises.
What Services Does Lady Jane Offer?
Lady Jane handles everything from last wills and living trusts to powers of attorney, healthcare directives, beneficiary designations, guardianship planning, and annual plan reviews. Whether you're just starting out or updating an existing plan, we have you covered.
How Do I Choose an Estate Planning Attorney?
Look for someone who specializes in estate planning, communicates clearly, and makes you feel comfortable. Lady Jane's approach is built on trust, transparency, and a genuine commitment to protecting your family — not billable hours or legal jargon.
When Should I Update My Estate Plan?
Major life changes — marriage, divorce, new children, property purchases, or relocation — are all good reasons to review your plan. We recommend an annual check-in to make sure everything stays current and aligned with your wishes.
