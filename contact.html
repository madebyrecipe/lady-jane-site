What is the estate planning process?
Our process begins with a discovery call to understand your family's needs. From there, we develop a customized strategy, draft your documents, and walk you through every detail before finalizing — all within a clear timeline.
How much does estate planning cost?
Costs vary based on the complexity of your situation. A basic will may start at a few hundred dollars, while comprehensive trust-based plans range from $2,000 to $5,000+. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
What services does Lady Jane offer?
We specialize in wills, living trusts, powers of attorney, healthcare directives, trust administration, probate guidance, succession planning, and guardianship designations — everything your family needs to feel protected.
How do I choose the right plan?
It starts with a conversation. During your free consultation, Tiana will assess your situation and recommend the plan that fits your family's needs, goals, and budget — no pressure, no upselling.
What is estate planning?
Estate planning is the process of arranging for the management and distribution of your assets during your lifetime and after death, ensuring your wishes are carried out and your loved ones are protected.
